About Me

I grew up in Oak Park, IL, 9 miles west of Chicago, and fell in love with space while visiting Adler Planetarium. Currently, I am a 4th year astrophysics major at Washington University in St. Louis (WashU) intending to pursue a Physics PhD.

I work in Dr. Manel Errando's experimental high-energy astrophysics lab where I research Active Galactic Nuclei . I specialize in X-ray data analysis and focus specifically on the relativistic jets generated by BL Lacertae (Bl Lac) objects.
